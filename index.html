Case Study WellSky · Specialty Care

The division's largest revenue-cycle migration

Senior Solutions Analyst at WellSky, and the only solution analyst on the program. I was the hub between Solutions, Engineering, Professional Services, PM, and the client.

Challenge

A Fortune 1000 post-acute and home-care provider had to move its Rehab, Community Living, and HomeCare billing off a legacy system onto WellSky Specialty Care. Requirements changed week to week, and some reporting definitions weren't settled until late.

What I did

  • Owned the scope end to end. I wrote 1,309 Jira stories and sequenced the work across several scrum teams.
  • Defined the hour-based authorization and reimbursement engine (day, week, and total-hour limits across PER15, PERHR, and Step-Down-by-Minutes). It carried an 8-wave go-live.
  • Specified a real-time authorization API with 18+ field mappings and two-way sync to Resource Manager.
  • Built 50+ bill exceptions across CMS-1500 and UB04, 3 new bill forms, and the GL and month-end reporting. Moved 8+ test features to Playwright for go-live.

Impact

200+ programs, 15+ financial and compliance reports, and 3 bill forms, all built from scratch. It runs high-volume, mixed-model authorization billing across an 8-wave cutover.
